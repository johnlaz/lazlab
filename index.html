01 · The Manifesto

A small studio
that ships like a fleet.

RULES OF THE HOUSE
EST. 2024

LAZLAB exists to prove that one developer with sharp taste and a refusal to cut corners can outship rooms full of frameworks.

Every product is a single file — no build steps, no dependencies that rot, no excuses. The studio is private. The output is public.

01

No simulated data.

Real APIs or no feature. Mock data is a lie shipped to production.

02

Surgical fixes.

Rewrites are a tax on the user. Patch with a scalpel, not a backhoe.

03

Local-first.

Your data stays on your device. localStorage, IndexedDB, no servers begging.

04

Validate before ship.

node --check on every delivery. Syntax errors are inexcusable.

04 · The Stack

LAZZARO STANDARD
SPECIFICATION

Single-File HTML

One file per app. HTML, CSS, vanilla JS, all inline. No bundler. No package.json that rots over a weekend.

DEPLOYS · GITHUB PAGES

Local-First Persistence

localStorage for small surfaces, IndexedDB when it matters. Your data on your device.

PRINCIPLE · 03

Groq · Llama 3.3 70B

Default reasoning engine across the portfolio. Fast, capable, predictable. llama-3.3-70b-versatile.

MODEL · 70B-VERSATILE

node --check, every ship

Syntax validation is a non-negotiable pre-flight. The release that breaks on load is the release that wasn't shipped.

PRINCIPLE · 04

The Lazzaro Standard

Pitch-black ground. Magenta brain. Cyan wordmark. Inter for body. Space Mono for the data. Bloom on every neon.

DESIGN · v2.0

PWA, installable

Every shipped product installs to home screen. Offline-tolerant. Service workers when the surface earns it.
